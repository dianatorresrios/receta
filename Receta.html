Chinese fried rice

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups cooked white rice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok on medium-high heat.
  2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic and cook for 2-3 minutes until softened.
  3. Add the frozen peas and carrots and continue cooking for another 2-3 minutes until vegetables are heated through.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the beaten eggs to the other side. Scramble the eggs until they are set and then mix them with the vegetables.
  5. Add the cooked white rice to the skillet and stir to combine with the vegetables and eggs. Pour the soy sauce over the rice mixture and stir to coat evenly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Cook for an additional 3-5 minutes until the rice is heated through and slightly crispy.
  8. Once everything is heated through and mixed together, add the chicken back to the wok or frying pan and stir-fry until everything is hot.
  9. Serve hot as a main dish or as a side with your favorite Asian-inspired meal. Enjoy!/li>
